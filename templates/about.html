About Botanica

Ireland's premier online plant shop, bringing nature to your doorstep since 2020.

Our Story

Botanica was born from a simple passion for plants and a desire to make quality greenery accessible to everyone in Ireland. What started as a small family business has grown into one of Ireland's most trusted online plant retailers.

Our journey began in 2020 when our founder, Albert Chan, decided to turn his lifelong passion for horticulture into a business that could bring joy and natural beauty to homes across Ireland.

Today, we pride ourselves on offering a carefully curated selection of indoor and outdoor plants, delivered with care right to your doorstep.

Our Mission

To bring the joy and benefits of plants to every Irish home, while promoting sustainable and eco-friendly practices.

Quality

We source only the healthiest plants and ensure they're delivered in perfect condition.

Sustainability

Our eco-friendly packaging and growing practices minimize our environmental impact.

Community

We support local growers and give back to environmental conservation efforts in Ireland.
